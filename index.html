Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AIdea

How does AIdea's AI work?

Our AI uses advanced natural language processing to understand your requirements, then scans through thousands of properties across 150+ data sources. It analyzes market trends, predicts ROI, and matches properties based on your specific needs - all in real-time through WhatsApp.

Is there any cost to use AIdea?

No! AIdea is completely free to use. We earn our commission from developers and sellers when you successfully purchase a property. There are no hidden fees, subscription costs, or charges for using our AI service.

Can I really communicate in my native language?

Absolutely! AIdea supports 15+ languages including Arabic, Hindi, Russian, Chinese, Tagalog, French, and more. You can type or send voice notes in your preferred language, and our AI will understand and respond naturally in the same language.

How accurate are the property recommendations?

Our AI achieves 98.7% accuracy in matching properties to client requirements. It continuously learns from successful matches and user feedback, improving its recommendations over time. The AI considers not just your stated preferences but also understands context and unstated needs.

Can AIdea help with investment analysis?

Yes! Our AI provides comprehensive investment analysis including rental yield predictions, capital appreciation forecasts, ROI calculations, and market timing insights. It also checks your eligibility for UAE residency visas based on your investment amount.
